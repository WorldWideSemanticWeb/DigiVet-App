Outcome: Newcastle Disease

It seems that your bird has a form of Newcastle Disease. This is a highly contagious disease that attacks the organs. It affects birds of all ages and humans are also susceptible to it. The Newcastle virus can be transmitted short distances by the airborne route or introduced on contaminated shoes, caretakers, tires or dirty equipment. Antibiotics can be given for 3-5 days to prevent seconary bacterial infections.
